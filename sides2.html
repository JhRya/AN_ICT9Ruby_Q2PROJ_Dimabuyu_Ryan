Fried Rice Recipe

Brief Background

Fried Rice is a staple Chinese dish made with stir-fried rice, vegetables, eggs, and seasonings. It’s a great way to use leftover rice and can be customized with your favorite proteins.

Ingredients

For the Fried Rice
  • 3 cups cooked and cooled rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

Prepare the Eggs:
  1. In a large skillet or wok over medium heat, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and scramble them until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
Stir-Fry the Vegetables:
  1. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  2. Add the diced carrots and stir-fry for 2 minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Add the frozen peas and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
Add the Rice and Seasonings:
  1. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes, breaking up any clumps.
  2. Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Add the scrambled eggs back to the skillet and toss everything together.
Serve:
  1. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
Preparation Time
  • 10 minutes
Cooking Time
  • 15 minutes
Servings
  • 4 servings
Notes
  • You can add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein.
Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 10g
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
