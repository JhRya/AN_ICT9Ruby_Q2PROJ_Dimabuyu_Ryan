Orange Chicken Recipe

Brief Background

Orange Chicken is a sweet and tangy Chinese-American dish made with crispy fried chicken bites coated in a flavorful orange glaze. It's a favorite takeout dish, known for its vibrant citrus flavor and crunchy texture.

Ingredients

For the Chicken
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Vegetable oil for frying
For the Orange Sauce
  • 1/2 cup orange juice (freshly squeezed or store-bought)
  • 1 tablespoon orange zest
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch mixed with 2 tablespoons water (slurry)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the Chicken:
  1. In a large bowl, combine cornstarch, flour, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Dip the chicken pieces in the beaten egg, then coat them in the flour mixture.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large skillet to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Fry the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per batch. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
Prepare the Orange Sauce:
  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine orange juice, orange zest, soy sauce, sugar, rice vinegar, ginger, garlic, and red chili flakes (if using).
  2. Bring the mixture to a simmer, stirring occasionally.
  3. Once simmering, add the cornstarch slurry and stir continuously until the sauce thickens.
  4. Remove the sauce from heat.
Toss the Chicken:
  1. In a large bowl, add the fried chicken pieces and pour the orange sauce over them.
  2. Toss gently to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce.
Serve:
  1. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions and sesame seeds, if desired.
Preparation Time
  • 20 minutes
Cooking Time
  • 20 minutes
Servings
  • 4 servings
Notes
  • For a healthier option, you can bake or air-fry the chicken instead of deep-frying.
  • Adjust the sweetness and tanginess of the sauce by varying the amount of sugar and rice vinegar.
Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 25g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 40g
