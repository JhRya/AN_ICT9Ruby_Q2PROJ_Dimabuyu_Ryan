Chicken Egg Roll Recipe

Brief Background

Chicken Egg Rolls are a popular Asian appetizer, featuring a crispy wrapper filled with seasoned chicken, vegetables, and savory spices. They are perfect for parties, snacks, or as a part of a meal.

Ingredients

For the Filling
  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped green onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
For the Wrappers
  • 12-15 egg roll wrappers
For Cooking
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Water for sealing wrappers
For the Dipping Sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped green onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the Filling:
  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground chicken until browned.
  2. Add cabbage, carrots, green onions, garlic, and ginger. Stir and cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and black pepper. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let the filling cool slightly.
Assemble the Egg Rolls:
  1. Place an egg roll wrapper on a clean surface with one corner pointing towards you (diamond shape).
  2. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of filling onto the center of the wrapper.
  3. Fold the bottom corner over the filling, then fold in the sides, and roll up tightly.
  4. Moisten the top corner with water to seal the egg roll.
  5. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
Cook the Egg Rolls:
  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Fry the egg rolls in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
Prepare the Dipping Sauce:
  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, green onions, and red chili flakes.
  2. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
Serve:
  1. Serve the egg rolls hot with the dipping sauce on the side.
Preparation Time
  • 25 minutes
Cooking Time
  • 15 minutes
Servings
  • 4 servings
Notes
  • You can freeze uncooked egg rolls on a baking sheet, then transfer them to a freezer bag. Fry from frozen by adding a few extra minutes to the cooking time.
  • Adjust the dipping sauce ingredients to taste.
Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 20g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
