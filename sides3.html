Super Greens Recipe

Brief Background

Super Greens is a healthy side dish made with a mix of steamed or stir-fried greens such as broccoli, kale, and cabbage. It’s a nutritious addition to any meal, offering vibrant colors and fresh flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

Steam the Greens:
  1. In a large pot of boiling water, steam the broccoli florets for 3 minutes until bright green and slightly tender. Remove and set aside.
Stir-Fry:
  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat the vegetable oil.
  2. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped kale and shredded cabbage. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly wilted.
  4. Add the steamed broccoli to the skillet.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and black pepper. Toss everything together until well combined.
Serve:
  1. Serve hot as a healthy side dish.
Preparation Time
  • 5 minutes
Cooking Time
  • 10 minutes
Servings
  • 4 servings
Notes
  • You can add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra freshness.
  • Feel free to substitute or add other greens like spinach or bok choy.
Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
  • Calories: 150
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 7g
  • Carbohydrates: 18g
