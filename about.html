Our Story

Panda Express, founded in 1983, is known for bringing bold, delicious Chinese-inspired cuisine to the world. Starting as a family business, it has grown into an international brand with thousands of locations across the globe. Panda Express has always focused on providing quality food made with fresh ingredients.

Our Mission & Values

At Panda Express, we believe in creating a world where good food brings people together. Our core values are:

  • Genuine Care: We care for our people, our customers, and the communities we serve.
  • Continuous Improvement: We strive to improve every day and innovate to deliver the best dining experience.
  • Giving Back: Through our philanthropic initiatives, we contribute to a better future.

Our Signature Flavors

At Panda Express, we take pride in delivering bold, unforgettable flavors inspired by authentic Chinese cuisine. Our carefully crafted recipes bring a fusion of savory, sweet, and tangy tastes to every bite. Whether you're dining in or taking out, you're guaranteed a delicious experience filled with fresh ingredients and mouthwatering sauces.
