Cream Cheese Rangoon Recipe

Brief Background

Cream Cheese Rangoons are a popular appetizer, featuring crispy wonton wrappers filled with a creamy cheese mixture. These are perfect for parties, snacks, or as a side dish.

Ingredients

For the Filling
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
For the Wrappers
  • 20-24 wonton wrappers
For Cooking
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Water for sealing wrappers
For the Dipping Sauce
  • 1/4 cup sweet and sour sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions

Prepare the Filling:
  1. In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, green onions, garlic powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Mix until smooth and well blended.
Assemble the Rangoons:
  1. Place a wonton wrapper on a clean surface.
  2. Spoon about 1 teaspoon of filling into the center of the wrapper.
  3. Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water.
  4. Fold the wrapper diagonally to form a triangle and press the edges to seal, ensuring there are no air bubbles.
  5. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
Cook the Rangoons:
  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Fry the rangoons in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Drain on a paper towel-lined plate.
Prepare the Dipping Sauce:
  1. In a small bowl, combine sweet and sour sauce, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  2. Stir until well mixed.
Serve:
  1. Serve the rangoons hot with the dipping sauce on the side.
Preparation Time
  • 20 minutes
Cooking Time
  • 10 minutes
Servings
  • 4 servings
Notes
  • For a sweeter filling, you can add more sugar to taste.
  • Ensure the oil is hot enough before frying to get crispy rangoons.
Nutritional Information (Per Serving)
  • Calories: 250
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
